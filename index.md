# Privacy Policy for Interrupt

Last updated: 02/24/2026

Interrupt is designed to respect your privacy.

## Information We Collect

Interrupt does not collect personal information such as name, email address, or location.

All reminders and settings you create are stored locally on your device.

## Notifications

Interrupt sends local notifications based on time windows you choose. These notifications are generated on your device and are not sent from a server.

## Data Storage

All reminder content and preferences are stored locally on your device using secure local storage.

We do not transmit your reminder content to external servers.

## Third-Party Services

If you choose to subscribe, payments are processed securely through Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not have access to your payment details.

## Analytics

In future versions, minimal anonymous analytics may be used to improve app performance. No personal reminder content will be collected.
